### Role of Customer in Look4KOL?

The customer account in look4kol has features like [**Recommendation Tools**](../recommendation-tools/)**,** [**Project Tools**](../project-tools.md)**,** [**Search Page**](https://www.look4kol.com/en/s)**,** and KOL Compare tools. These tools are excellent tools and help customer brands increase sales and their influencer marketing campaign ROIs.

Customers can direct message KOLs by finding them from the search page and creating a contract with them from the chatroom or the recommendation tools.
